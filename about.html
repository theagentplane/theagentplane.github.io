About

Two engineers, building the layer agents need underneath them.

AgentPlane is an open-source startup co-founded by Susheem Koul and Tisha Chawla. We build infrastructure for production AI agents and publish the work in the open.

AgentPlane is an independent company. It is not affiliated with or endorsed by Microsoft. Founders' employer titles are listed for identification only.
