T

TokenOps

View repo ↗

A control plane alongside your agent (data plane). TokenOps measures spend as it accrues, attributes it to the right run and customer context, and enforces deterministic policies — before and after each model, tool, and delegation call. Gateways see requests; TokenOps governs the full run: loops, growing context, fan-out, and runaway cost.
